Retail Analytics

Pricing & Profitability Analysis

Problem: The business lacked visibility on how discount strategies impact profitability and sales performance.

What I did

  • Analysed relationships between sales, profit, and discounts
  • Identified unprofitable products and categories
  • Compared performance across categories and time
  • Built visualisations to explore pricing patterns
  • Developed an interactive dashboard

Result

  • Found that high discounts often reduce profit margins
  • Showed that sales do not always increase with higher discounts
  • Identified profit leakage caused by discounting
  • Provided recommendations to optimise pricing strategy
  • Enabled better decision-making on discounts and margins
Tools: Python, pandas, scikit-learn, Power BI

Predictive Analytics

Crime Risk Analysis & Prediction

Problem: Crime data from multiple years was inconsistent and difficult to analyse, limiting the ability to identify trends, high-risk areas, and support decision-making for local authorities.

What I did

  • Cleaned and merged multi-year crime datasets (2022–2024)
  • Removed missing values, duplicates, and geographic outliers
  • Applied reverse geocoding to improve location accuracy
  • Engineered time-based features and enriched data with socio-economic indicators
  • Performed EDA and clustering to detect crime zones
  • Built a Random Forest model and interactive Streamlit dashboard

Result

  • Identified seasonal peaks in July and December
  • Highlighted hotspots such as Dogs Head Street and Lloyds Avenue
  • Showed that violence & sexual offences were the most frequent crimes
  • Built a predictive model with 46–48% accuracy and F1 = 0.39
  • Delivered data-driven insights for local crime prevention strategies
Tools: Python, pandas, scikit-learn, Plotly, Folium, Streamlit, Power Query
